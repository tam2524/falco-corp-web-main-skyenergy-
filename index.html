Overview

Falco Corporation is venturing into new markets to connect with customers anytime and anywhere.

Founded in the United Arab Emirates in 2021, Falco Corporation has evolved into a leading player in the oil and energy sector. We embrace innovation, sustainability, and excellence to compete on the global stage.

Providing Diversified Energy Solutions

Our commitment to innovation is reflected in our diverse product portfolio. Starting historically with crude oil, we have consistently expanded our offerings to include a range of essential energy products like Fuel Oil, LPG, and Diesel, all designed to meet the highest industry standards.

Becoming a Global Top-Tier Company in the Energy Sector

Falco Corporation is committed to company-wide innovations and strategic growth. By exploring unique partnerships and actively evolving our business model, we are positioned as a top energy company with a strong presence in Europe, the US, Singapore, China, and more.
